🌽 How it works
🌽 The Vault

Tap Harvest a Wallet to mint a brand-new XRPL wallet — you get the address, the family seed, and a 24-word phrase. Seed and words stay blurred until you tap them.

🖊️ Backing up

Your family seed and 24 words are the wallet — write them down and keep them somewhere safe offline. They import into any XRPL wallet (like Xaman), so that's all you ever need. There are no files to save and nothing is stored on your device.

🎯 The Harvest Engine

Pick where your word should land — Front, Middle, Back, or a full Pattern — type it, and hit Engage Turbines. The engine spins fresh wallets until one matches, then drops it in the Harvest Bin to copy or bank into the Vault.

🎲 Odds & speed

Case-insensitive (on by default) finds matches much faster. The odds line estimates how rare your word is — short words land in seconds, long ones can take a long time. Turbines = how many CPU threads hunt at once.

🌽 Your keys are yours

Everything is generated right here in your browser — nothing is ever uploaded. The seed and 24 words are full control of the wallet, so keep them private and back them up somewhere safe.
